Product Details

1. Product overview:


A new type of smart card water meter with simple structure and reliable work; it adopts patented waterproof technology and has super waterproof performance; patented appearance technology is more in line with modern living environment; easy to achieve step water price; card insertion and swiping in one, stable performance; card The user-friendly design of the slot ensures that the user can swipe the card accurately and effectively; the high-power valve design ensures the smooth opening and closing of the valve and avoids the valve from jamming. It is widely used in residential areas, office buildings, rural reconstruction and other places.


The sensor area of the smart card water meter is a functional measuring instrument composed of a family home water meter with a signaling device as the measurement base meter, the family as a media, and an electronic controller and an electric ball valve. The circuit adopts high performance , The high-efficiency RISC structure single-chip microcomputer integrates various control circuits such as the controller's clock, liquid crystal drive, alarm drive and pause and wake-up functions into a dedicated module with a highly integrated method, which makes the family water meter have a very high The performance of anti-interference, reliability, high precision and low power consumption, compact structure design and beautiful appearance.
