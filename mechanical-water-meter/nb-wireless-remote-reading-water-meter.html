nb water meter product Features

1.Periodic report: report the 24 hours data of the previous day, and the data includes daily freezinginformation.

2.High-frequency periodic reporting: The sending frequency can be set or changed through the platform

and the minimum reporting period is every hour

3.Intensive data collection:4 hours from the set start time, one point every 5 minutes, a total of 48 points

of accumulated flow.

4.Data repackaging: When data reporting is unsuccessful, the data will be automatically re-reported inthe next reporting period, and data within the effective storage range can be re-reported.

5. Discrete setting: Discrete the reported time points randomly through the program, the minimumestimated length is 10S, and the default is 0 to 8 points to ensure that the communication basestation will not be blocked by a large amount of data in a short time.

6.Remote setting of parameters: Through the near-end handheld infrared reading device and

communicating with the management platform, setting IP, port and other parameters

7. Alarm function: When the battery undervoltage is lower than the predetermined value, the platform

will automatically alarm to remind the administrator

8. Time proofreading: When the data is reported periodically, the clock will be automatically proofread

with the platform to ensure the accuracy of the time
