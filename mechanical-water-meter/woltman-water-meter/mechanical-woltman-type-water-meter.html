Mechanical Woltman Type Water Meter

Product Details

Features:


1. Non-removable water meter: mainly used in agricultural irrigation and industrial meters. It has the following advantages: novel structure and stable performance; stable and reliable transmission performance; it can measure the water quality of hard water, scale, and suspended particles; small pressure loss, intuitive reading and long service life; remote transmission can be provided according to needs Device.
