Woltman Type Water Meters

This woltman water meter uses a wheel-type mechanical counter with a compact structure and clear and intuitive readings.

Send Inquiry
Product Details

Features:


1. The whole meter of the woltman water meter is composed of a housing, a measuring mechanism, an indicating device and the like.


2. The water meter consists of a right-angle or horizontal meter housing, an impeller measuring machine, an adjustment mechanism, a magnetic transmission mechanism, and an indicating mechanism.


3. The connection method of the water meter is flange connection.


4. The water meter uses a magnetic coupling to rotate the device. There is no reduction gear in the high-pressure cavity to avoid the phenomenon of gear jamming due to poor water quality.


5. Ensure the accuracy of the measurement and the sensitivity of the water meter to improve the service life. This type of meter adopts a structured wheel-type mechanical counter, which has a compact structure and a clear and intuitive reading.


6. The water meter adopts an all-mechanical structure design, which makes this type of water meter have a strong applicability to the harsh working environment. The product is suitable for places with harsh working environments such as mining and industrial wastewater treatment.
