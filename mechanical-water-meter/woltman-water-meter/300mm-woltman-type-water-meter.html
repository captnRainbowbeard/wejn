Product Details

Agricultural irrigation Large-diameter woltman water meters are water meters that not only protect water resources but also water. It effectively combines irrigation and water conservation into the operational farmland irrigation equipment system.


Woltman water meter can be used not only for farmland irrigation, but also for large water sales units such as water plants, water plants, markets, water stations, etc. It is reliable and easy to manage.
