With economic development and global population growth, coupled with the impact of climate change, our demand for water is increasing. Therefore, we urgently need to develop a sustainable and scientific smart water system to use every drop of water correctly.

The development trend of intelligent irrigation system is mainly manifested in the following aspects


1. Smart irrigation

Intelligent irrigation is to choose the irrigation method according to the actual needs of the crops and the moisture in the soil. Improving environmental sustainability is the main advantage of smart irrigation. By optimizing water consumption, pumping energy can also be reduced.


2. Data collection and analysis

Intelligent irrigation must be based on a large amount of data collection and analysis, so we need a software platform that can read and store data in real time
