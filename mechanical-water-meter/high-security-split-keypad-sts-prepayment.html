High Security Split Keypad Sts Prepayment Water Meter

Product Details

An integrated keyboard can be used to enter a recharge token and check information through short code.

Stronpay can be integrated into local mobile money payment platforms such as M-PESa, Alipay, MTN, Airtel, etc.Users can recharge PAYG water with mobile money without leaving their homes.

Pay-as-you-go features and excellent tamper-proof features such as magnetic protection can help utilities achieve revenue water protection and consumer management.

A customer interface unit (CIu) is provided to enter recharge tokens and information through short code.

The ClU will be installed in the customer's house when the meter is located away from the customer in a lockable box or in the house.
