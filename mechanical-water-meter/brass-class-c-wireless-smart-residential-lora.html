Brass Class C Wireless Smart Residential Lora Water Meter

It is mainly used to measure and monitor the flow of water medium, such as domestic water, central air conditioning system water, equipment pipe network water, etc. Its accuracy is higher than normal, can reach R200, accurate measurement.

Product Details

Wireless Remote Ultrasonic Water Meter (LoRa) is an intelligent measuring instrument consisting of a flow sensor, a calculator and a wireless LoRa transmission module.

The calculator takes accurate measurements of the flow by processing the temperature difference collected by the sensor and the time difference between the sound waves passing through the fluid, and wirelessly transmits data through the LoRa module.

All parts of the flowmeter (including flow sensor parts and calculator parts) shall be installed in a position to avoid exposure, water injection, freezing, chemical and electromagnetic contamination and to facilitate disassembly and reading.

When the working time of the ultrasonic water meter is 6 years or less, but the data displayed on the display screen is not clear or indicating low power (please refer to the display instructions), the battery should be replaced by professional technicians to avoid affecting the normal operation of the water meter.
