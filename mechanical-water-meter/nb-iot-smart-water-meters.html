Product Details

The water consumption data, electricity quantity, signal and abnormal conditions of intelligent water meter can be connected to NB IOT network through NB IOT communication module built in water meter, transmitted to IOT connection management platform, and then uploaded to the background collection and business system platform

the background system platform will analyze the data packet, and the resolved user gas data will be settled in the user account, which will be sent through the customer service system Users can get their water bills in real time and recharge their accounts remotely.
