Mechanical Household Class C Single Jet Water Meter

Product Details

The mechanical single water jet operates according to the speed principle, in which water enters the measuring chamber through the inlet and drives the impeller. The movement is transmitted via gears and magnetic coupling to a register that records the readings. The single water jet has a dry dial with a diameter of 15mm~25mm. It can reach the class c and is suitable for household use.


Features :

Size:1/2", 3/4", 1"

Body Material: plastic,brass

Dial type: dry dial

Accurate reading, accurate measurement

Tamper - Resistant Construction

The magnetic drive is unaffected by external magnetic interference

Every water meter will undergo strict QC inspection before leaving the factory.
