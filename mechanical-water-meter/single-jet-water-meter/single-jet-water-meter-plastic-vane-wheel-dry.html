Features


· Single-jet, dry-dial, free rotating register, small in size and light in weight.

· Keep the reading clear in a long term service.


Working Conditions


· Water temperature: 0.1℃～50℃.


· Water pressure: ≤1.0Mpa.

· Available with different length on request.

· Register sealed: Dry-dial only.


· Size: 15-20mm for Brass body, 15-20 for plastic body.


· Cold/Hot water.


· Non-return valve.


· Reed switch option.


· Thread end type: BSP/NPT.

1. The Counting Part

 

（1）Register: POM (polyformaldehyde) material, porcelain white color，which ensures the pure material production, without adding any waste, and with strong wear resistance.

 

 

（2）Central Gear: Vertical design to ensure its coaxality.


2、The Measuring Part


（1）Turbine Component: It is made of stainless steel, inset bushings and corundum for improved wear resistance.


（2）Measuring Chamber: The mould is made of high quality material, which ensures small error and high pass rate.


In addition to accidents, every 1 million sets of molds produced will be replaced to ensure high precision.


（3）Bottom Strainer: The bottom filter is designed to minimize the impact of the filter on accuracy.
