20mm Multijet Water Meter

The multi jet water meter is mainly composed of a casing, an impeller measuring mechanism, and an indicator meter.

Product Details

The multi jet water meter is a meter that records the water consumption of tap water. It is installed on the water pipe. When the user releases water, the pointer or character wheel on the meter rotates to indicate the amount of water passing.

The bearer- Body part:

1.Brass Tail piece: The copper content of raw materials is 59%, the lowest value is

57%, and the copper content is 55%-56%, which is prone to fracture.

2.Body:The average wall thickness is 2.5-2.7mm

3.Coupling Nut: Guarantee its length and weight, the weight will not be lower than

140g, otherwise it will be easy to break.

Reed switch part :

1.It is the first-grade product imported from OKI Japan ( total of 4 grades).

2.Humanized design: fixed with screws, easy to replace. Defective products in the market are disposable and cannot be replaced. With double reed switch design, if one of them is damaged, it can still work normally.

3.It performed three tests. Store in the factory for 2 months, and eliminate the internal air leakage and damage;During installation, it will be tested again. Later,it will be spot-checked.
