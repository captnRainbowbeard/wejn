Central Gear


The center gear is equipped with four samarium cobalt magnets,which are not easy to weathering,degaussing and losing data.This ensures the life of the central transmission part of the water meter and the accuracy of measurement.


Dial Cover


The counter disc is made of POM, white porcelain, clear reading and durable service life. Fixed with screws,easy to replace.With double reed switch design, if one of them is damaged,it can still work normally.


Anti Magnetic Ring


When the reed switch is installed,the anti-magnetic ring is added, the pig iron material is required, and the signal strength of the magnet steel is required to be 3,000-4,000 gauss,which ensures the stable output of the pulse signal.


Turbine Component


The impeller shaft is made of stainless steel, agate, bushing and corundum.The service life and accuracy are guaranteed.


Transmission Gear


High precision die,1 million sets will be replaced to ensure small error,high throughput, and ensure the accuracy of water meters and delivery time.
