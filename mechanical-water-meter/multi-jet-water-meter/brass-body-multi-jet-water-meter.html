Product Details

The multi-flow meter is a meter that records the water consumption of the tap water and is installed on the water pipe. When the user releases the water, the pointer or the character wheel on the watch rotates to indicate the amount of water passing through.

Features:

1. Magnetic drive with anti-magnetic function

2. Divided into multiple streams in the water meter

3. Water meter uses high quality materials to make performance more stable and reliable

4. The counter is vacuum sealed, anti-condensation atomization, and can keep readings clear for a long time.

5. Measurement accuracy can reach ISO 4064 Class B standard

6. The water meter has strong versatility and is easy to replace and repair.
