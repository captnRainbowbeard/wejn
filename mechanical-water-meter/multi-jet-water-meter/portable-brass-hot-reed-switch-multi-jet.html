Portable Brass Hot Reed Switch Multi Jet Water Meter

Send Inquiry
Product Details

Water meter work principle:

The multi-jet water meter has a plurality of water inlets in the inner cavity, and a plurality of water jets abut against the impeller. While the impeller rotates, the impeller teeth are transmitted to the gear reduction mechanism, and finally the pointer is driven to indicate the total amount flowing through the water meter.


Quick details:

Item name: DN15-DN50 multi jet brass water meter.

Measuring medium: hot water, the maximum temperature is 90 degrees;

Accuracy: Class B, R80/100

Thread end type: BSP/NPT

Faced with difficult reading conditions, the water meter can be equipped with a reed switch, the line length can be customized, user-friendly design, easy to replace with screws.


The advantages are as follows:

Large flow resistance;

Long lasting

Less sensitive to installation conditions
