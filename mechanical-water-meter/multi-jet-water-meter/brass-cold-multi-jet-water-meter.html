Brass Cold Multi Jet Water Meter

Domestic rotary wing water meter refers to a water meter with a plurality of radial rotary wings placed on a rotor whose rotation axis is perpendicular to the direction of water flow.

Product Details

Applications:


The wing water meter is widely used in the flow measurement of small-caliber pipes. Rotary wing water meter can meet the caliber use of general household water meters. Rotor-type water meters have a diameter of DN15 ~ DN40. The water meter industry uses LXS to indicate rotary wing wet water meters.


Rotary wing water meter is used to measure small-caliber pipes, which is related to the structure of the water meter movement. The rotating shaft of the rotor type water meter impeller is perpendicular to the direction of the water flow, the area where the water flow contacts the meter body is small, the initial flow through the water meter is small, and the corresponding measurement range is also small. Therefore, it is mostly used to measure smaller flows. It has the characteristics of high accuracy, strong anti-interference ability and strong reliability.
