Product Details

Rotor liquid seal type multi-jet water meter, this type of water meters is used for measuring the amount of drinking water flowing through the water pipe for the family or a residential unit. The digital wheel of the counter is packaged with special liquid, which can keep the reading clear for a long time and is not affected by water quality. Particularly suitable for use in areas with poor water quality and tight water resources. The items can increase impulse output according to user requirements.

Size from DN15mm-50mm

Thread end with 1 set of connectors.

Technical data conform to international standard.


Liquid Sealed Multi Jet Impulse Output Water Meter Conditions of Use:

Water temperature: cold type is not higher than 40 °C (the hot type is not higher than 90 °C) and does not lower than 0 ° C

Pressure: ≤1MPa
