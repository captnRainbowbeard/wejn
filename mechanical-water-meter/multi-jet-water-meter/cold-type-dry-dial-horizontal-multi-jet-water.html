Product Details

Multi-jet rotor type cold water meter is a measure of the total amount of one-way water flow to the pipe. It has the characteristics of simple structure and more accurate measurement. Multi jet cold water meters can also be divided into dry dial and wet type, and the dry water meter reads clearly. The desiccant is built into the counter to keep the dial clear for a long time.


Optional Features

Size: 1/2" - 2”

Register sealed types: Super-dry type, dry type, wet type

Check valve

Pulse output

Clod / hot type

Trademark: SH-MECH/ Neutral trademark/ Customer settings


Installation matters:

Please note that there is an "H-B" mark in the dial, which means horizontal installation, the accuracy is class B.
