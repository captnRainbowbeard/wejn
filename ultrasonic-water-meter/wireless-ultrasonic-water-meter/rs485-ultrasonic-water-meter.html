Rs485 Ultrasonic Water Meter

Ultrasonic water meter is an instrument that measures water flow through ultrasonic sound beam, which can realize functions such as water loss alarm and greatly reduce water loss.

Send Inquiry
Product Details

The ultrasonic water meter adopts the principle of ultrasonic wave. By detecting the time difference caused by the change of the speed when the ultrasonic sound beam propagates in the water, the water flow rate is calculated by analyzing and processing the water flow rate.

Ultrasonic water meters have the following performance characteristics:

1. High precision: The measurement accuracy is generally within ±1%, and the error is more than 1 times that of mechanical water meter.

2. Low starting point: Ultrasonic water meter can detect tiny water flow, which can help the water supply department to effectively increase income.

3, low pressure loss: ultrasonic water meter has a cross-sectional structure of straight tubular measuring tube, no mechanical parts, reducing water flow barriers, reducing the cost of the water supply department.
