Product Details

The ultrasonic water meter adopts the newly developed ultrasonic propagation phase and time difference measurement technology, which has the remarkable characteristics of high accuracy, low power consumption, wide measurement range ratio, stability and reliability.In the structure, the component IP68 solution is adopted, that is, each component reaches the IP68 protection level, and the meter cavity can ensure long-term operation even if water enters. This series of water meters can be widely used in urban water supply pipelines, household water consumption total meters, water intake monitoring, farmland irrigation, and also suitable for various industrial sites.



Wireless ultrasonic water meter technology:


1. Wireless frequency self-calibration technology: effectively avoid the frequency deviation of the wireless signal due to temperature changes, and ensure the reliability of wireless signal reception.


2. Electromagnetic wave wake-up technology: The wireless water meter can automatically detect the meter reading signal when it is dormant, which reduces the power consumption of the water meter.


3. Hysteresis sampling technology: filtering and judging the sampling signal through the program, effectively preventing the sampling error caused by pipeline jitter.


4. Fully sealed design: All circuits are sealed with epoxy resin, no exposed electrodes, and good sealing, which ensures the long-term stable operation of the circuit board in a humid environment.
