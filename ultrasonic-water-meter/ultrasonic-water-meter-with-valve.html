Product Details

Ultrasonic water meter is to detect ultrasonic waves in the water downstream and countercurrent propagation time difference, This new all-electronic water meter is calculated by the program analysis and processing of water flow rate and then calculate the water flow.

Main Features

Strong adaptability to water quality: the ultrasonic water meter is not disturbed by impurities such as gravel, weeds, chemical substances and magnetic substances in the medium, so it can be said to fully adapt to the current water quality in China.

Good reliability: the structure and principle of ultrasonic water meter ensure that the water meter can work under high pressure, low temperature and water immersion for a long time without damage. Compared with the intelligent water meter based on mechanical water meter, it has stronger protection against external strong magnetic attack. In addition, the ultrasonic water meter can be recovered after external strong magnetic attack. Compared with other intelligent water meters, it will be permanently damaged after strong magnetic attack. The ultrasonic water meter has excellent performance.
