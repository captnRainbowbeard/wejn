Prepaid Water Meter With Ic Card

The water meter uses advanced microcomputer control technology and uses the IC card as the information carrier. The original "water use first, then payment" is changed to "pay first, then water use", which avoids the user's meter reading charges brought to users Many inconveniences.

Send Inquiry
Product Details

Features:


1. Long service life: using 3.6V high-energy lithium battery can reach more than 6 years. The battery compartment opening is above the water meter, which is convenient for users to replace the battery.


2. Prepaid water meter can realize the function of prepayment. Buy water first, then water, a new model of water management.


3. Water cut off alarm function: set the alarm water volume. When the alarm value is reached, the valve will close. At this time, you can use the remaining water by swiping the card. When the remaining water amount is zero, the user needs to purchase water to use the card.


4. Battery under pressure indication function: When the battery is low, it will display under pressure and close the valve automatically.


5. Anti-electromagnetic: When receiving electromagnetic interference, strong impact will automatically close the valve.


6. Abnormal valve closing function: When battery power failure or low voltage is detected, the valve will be automatically closed when the sensor fails, and the LCD screen will display a fault code.
