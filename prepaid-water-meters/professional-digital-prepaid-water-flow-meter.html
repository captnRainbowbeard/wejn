Professional Digital Prepaid Water Flow Meter with Smart Card

The prepaid water meter is new type of smart water meter that uses modern microelectronic technology, modern sensing technology, and smart IC card technology to measure water consumption and conduct water data transmission and settlement transactions.

Product Details

Professional Digital Prepaid Water Flow Meter with Smart Card can implement step-type water meters. When charging, managers may encounter some problems: when it is time to pay water fees, some people will not pay. Too many meters make reading difficult. Shmeters' pre-paid water meters can solve these problems. It allows managers to sit at home and easily collect water charges.



advantage:
1. Large screen LCD display for clear display at a glance
2. Fully encapsulated electronic glue technology, waterproof and moisture-proof
3. The valve adopts anti-lock suspension structure with low torque
4. Step water price, you can set up to five step water prices
5, large-capacity lithium battery warranty for more than 6 years
6. The data sheet can be sent to other management systems.
7. The water meter can only enter the user card to ensure that even if the user misreads the water meter, it will not cause loss.
