Product Details

The prepaid water meter system consists of a prepaid water meter, an IC card, a card reader and management software. Smart Ic Card Digital Prepaid Water Meter With Software adopts a one-meter one-card system.


Simply install the software on your computer and the card reader is connected to the computer via the USB interface to transfer data to the IC card. The user brings his IC card to the management center to pay the fee. After the payment is successful, the card is placed close to the water meter. After the card is successfully read, the water can be used.


Prepaid water flow meter use conditions:

Water temperature: 0-30℃

Working pressure: no more than 1MPa

Pressure loss class: ΔP63

Maximum allowable error of LoRa water meter: ±5% & ± 2%

Accuracy Class: Class B

Normal Diameter: DN15-DN600

Maximum indication: 99999

Flow prefile sensitivity class: U10/D5

Environmental Class: Class B


Advantage:

1. High Precision Water Measurement. Account balance is 0, water supply will be stopped at once

2. Language and Currency unit can be customized.

3. Different alarm system according to market demand.

4. Data sheet can be sent to other management system.

5. A water meter can only input a user card, which ensures that even if the user reads the water meter incorrectly not cause losses.
