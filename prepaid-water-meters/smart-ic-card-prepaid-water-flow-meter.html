Smart Ic Card Prepaid Water Flow Meter

Non-contact smart prepaid water meter is suitable for measuring the total volume of water flowing through the water supply pipeline.

Send Inquiry
Product Details

Product features:


1. IP68 protection level: The pre-paid smart water meter adopts the structure sealing design, the sealing performance reaches IP68, and it can work normally in humid environments.


2. Non-contact IC card technology: wireless data transmission, waterproof and moisture-proof.


3. Battery replacement function: Users can replace the battery by themselves. After replacing the battery, swipe the card to open the valve.


4. The pre-paid water meter has a step-by-step water price function: the property management department can set different water prices according to the actual situation of the water meter to achieve step-by-step water charges.


5. LED liquid crystal display: user number, remaining water, cumulative water consumption, valve status, battery status and other information.


6. Professionalism: The patented ball valve has low power consumption and small pressure loss.


7. Accuracy: Adopt patented double sampling technology, anti-magnetic and anti-interference, accurate measurement.
