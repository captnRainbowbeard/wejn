IC Card Prepaid Smart Water Meter

The use of smart IC card water meter is very simple. The user uses the smart IC card water meter to pay the water fee and inserts (or induces) the IC card into the water meter.

Product Details

The ic card prepaid smart water meter needs to be paid before water can be used. Solved the problem that the water department could not collect water fees.


From a management perspective. The IC card water meter should have some basic functions.


The function of one card for each household. Through the establishment of user password and user check code to achieve one card for each user. That is to say, a water meter can only use one user specific card, and other cards are invalid. This is helpful to improve the safety of water meter.


Card replacement function. The user's card may be lost. In this case, it can be replaced. At this time, there are two possibilities: one is lost when there is no amount in the card, the other is lost when there is amount in the card, both of which are considered. The card replacement is carried out through the management software.
