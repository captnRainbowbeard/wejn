Intelligent Prepaid Water Meter

Intelligent prepaid water meters play a very important role in the reliability, safety and water saving of water consumption information. The prepaid water meter can realize one household, one meter and one card.

Send Inquiry
Product Details

Intelligent prepaid water meter is made of food grade epoxy resin sealant, which is highly waterproof and anti-foaming. It is suitable for use in suburban outdoor, underground and outdoor environments.


Performance characteristics of prepaid water meters:

 

1. Non-contact technology, safe and reliable, can be used with confidence.


2. Patented waterproof fully-sealed design: all electronic circuits are sealed with silicone without any exposed electrodes. Can be immersed in water to work normally, completely solve the problems of waterproof and moisture proof.


3. Anti-attack and magnetic attack technology: When a strong electric or magnetic attack is performed from the outside, the water meter will automatically close the valve and indicate.


4. Microcomputer micro memory technology: highly integrated and highly reliable.
