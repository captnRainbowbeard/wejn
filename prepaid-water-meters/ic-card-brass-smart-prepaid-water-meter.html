Product Details

IC card prepaid water meter


The prepaid water meter is a smart water meter with both mechanical and electronic readings. It can control user`s overspend by prepaying for an IC card. It is suited to the metering and billing of urban residential water use, using water after fee paid that gets rid of the inconvenience of on-site visit for meter reading and tariffing .

-- Contactless radio frequency card technology, wireless data transmission, hermetically sealed, response       
    zone for IC card without card groove, tamperproof;
-- Valve use of anti-blockage suspension construction,water meter will periodically open and close the 
    valve., eliminates any risk of blockage ,improving the reliability and life span of the valve
-- Use of hermetically sealed construction design, silicone gel seal with IP68 protection
-- Use of drawer-type battery box construction, user can easily change the battery;
-- Regularly close/open the valve to remove the contaminant on the surface of the ball valve;
-- Ladder water price, It has three different pricing methods;
-- Is resistant to magnetic interference ,Magnetic interference protection, when the water meter encounters 
   external magnetic attack, it will automatically close the valve, and when the magnetic interference 
   disappears, it will open the valve again;
-- Lithium battery life ≥ 6 years.



Application

IC card prepaid water meter is the new measuring instrument employing the microprocessor to realize water collection ,processing, display and reserving. It is the best choice for realizing intelligent control by most of tap water administrative bureaus, Ideal for enhancing public awareness of saving water. This meter has many patents. Compared with traditional way, it can control user's overspend by prepaying for an IC card
