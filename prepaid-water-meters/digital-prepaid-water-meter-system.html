Digital Prepaid Water Meter System Installation

Product Details

The prepaid water meter system consists of a prepaid water meter, an IC card, a card reader and management software. Digital prepaid water meter system installation adopts a one-meter one-card system.


Simply install the software on your computer and the card reader is connected to the computer via the USB interface to transfer data to the IC card. The user brings his IC card to the management center to pay the fee. After the payment is successful, the card is placed close to the water meter. After the card is successfully read, the water can be used.


Product Features

1. Low start-up flow;

2. Base meter adopts of 4-digit pointer, 5-digit character roller for easy and clear reading;

3. Valve adopts rotary-wing construction, with small switch torture distance;

4. Long lifespan, reliable,integated meter body and valve, rational construction;


FAQ

1. How long is the battery life?

Using ER14505 battery, the capacity is up to 2700 mAh, the battery life can be up to 6-8 years.


2. The computer where the software is installed is broken. How to keep the system working?

In the process of using the system, user data need to be backed up. When replacing a new computer, the system can continue to use as long as the backup user data is imported.
