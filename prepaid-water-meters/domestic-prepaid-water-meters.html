Domestic Prepaid Water Meters

The prepayment meter is a new type of water meter that uses modern microelectronic technology, modern sensing technology, and smart IC card technology to achieve water measurement, water control, data transmission, and transaction settlement.

Send Inquiry
Product Details

Product features of domestic prepaid water meter:


(1) IP68 protection level: adopts structural seal design, the sealing performance reaches IP68, and can work normally in humid environment;


(2) Non-contact IC card technology: wireless data transmission, waterproof and moisture-proof;


(3) Ladder water price function: supports monthly and annual charges, and the user card updates the ladder water price parameters;


(4) Valve maintenance function: Regularly open and close the valve to prevent valve scaling;


(5) Valve abnormal closing function: When a battery power failure or low voltage is detected, the valve will automatically close when the sensor fails, and the LCD screen will display a fault code;


(6) Battery replacement function: The user can replace the battery of the prepaid water meter by himself. After replacing the battery, slide the user card to open the valve;


(7) Data copy function: The user card of the domestic prepaid water meter can bring water information and status words back to the meter;


(8) Anti-stagnant water meter function: If the user does not use water for a long time, the valve will automatically close, and the card can be opened by swiping the card.
