Product Details

The prepaid water meter is to facilitate the charging department to implement the policy of paying first and then using water. Users need to recharge before using water, and the water can be used after the recharge is completed. This reasonably limits the user's water consumption, saves water and avoids water resources.


Features:


1. Using patented waterproof technology, the waterproof level is very high in China.


2. Prepayment, automatic cumulative addition and reduction of water volume, and free water cut-off.


3. The quantity statistics are free, and the statistical method can be chosen arbitrarily.


4. Full Chinese LCD display, easy operation and strong practicability.


5. Low power consumption, anti-interference ability, and good hexagram.


6. One table with one card, one table with multiple cards, and one card with multiple tables in multiple formats.


7. In line with international calibration, logical encryption, multiple encryption.
