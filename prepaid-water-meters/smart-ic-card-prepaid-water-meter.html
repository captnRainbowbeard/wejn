Smart Ic Card Prepaid Water Meter

Product Details


The prepaid water meter system consists of a prepaid water meter, an IC card, a card reader and management software. Ic card smart prepaid domestic home water meter adopts a one-meter one-card system.

Advantage:


1. Pre-paid function: The amount of water is transferred in both directions through the IC card. After purchasing the water, the water is automatically removed, and the water is automatically shut off.

2, status display function: the user can visually view the information of the water meter, such as the water purchase amount, the remaining water volume, the accumulated water consumption, the on-off valve status, the power shortage reminder, etc., the display is clear and intuitive.

3. Hoarding limit function: When the recharge amount exceeds the limit value, the water meter will not read the purchase amount of water in the card until the sum of the remaining water quantity in the water meter and the purchase amount is less than the limit amount.

4. Fully sealed design: The water meter adopts two waterproof seals. The upper and lower shells are sealed by sealing gaskets. The circuit board is sealed with epoxy resin. The sealing effect is strong, moisture proof, waterproof and dustproof. stable.

Application field: This product is mainly used for metering and charging of water for residential buildings and enterprises. The water meter is an ideal metering and charging device for the modern management of water management departments such as water companies and real estate companies.
