Smart Prepaid Card Water Meter

The smart prepaid card water meter is a new type of water meter. It can automatically control the water consumption according to the agreement, and can store and remotely transmit the water consumption data, and at the same time realize the prepayment function.

Send Inquiry
Product Details

Features: 

     

1. The intelligent prepaid card water meter movement uses a dry water meter movement, which can ensure that the water meter word wheel display is permanent and clear, and is not affected by water quality.


2. The appearance of our products is fully sealed and waterproof, and the large LCD digital display is convenient for reading data.


3. The battery life of our water meter can be used for 6-8 years.


4. The base table shell of the prepaid card water meter is made of all copper, and the secondary electrostatic spraying anti-corrosion and anti-oxidation process is adopted, and the base table is not corroded.


5. The intelligent prepaid card water meter can automatically copy the data according to the requirements preset by the customer.


6. This smart water meter can realize remote monitoring function, pre-paid credit card function and ladder water price function.


7.One card per table is implemented. If the card is lost, there is no need to worry about the card being stolen.
