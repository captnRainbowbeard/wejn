Product Details

The prepaid water meter is new type of smart water meter that uses modern microelectronic technology, modern sensing technology, and smart IC card technology to measure water consumption and conduct water data transmission and settlement transactions. Smart prepaid system Brass body dry dial class b water meter adopts a pre-paid method, which not only achieves reasonable charging, it will also automatically close the valve when the meter is in arrears.


Prepaid water flow meter use conditions:

Water temperature: 0-30℃

Working pressure: no more than 1MPa

Pressure loss class: ΔP63

Maximum allowable error of LoRa water meter: ±5% & ± 2%

Accuracy Class: Class B

Normal Diameter: DN15-DN600

Maximum indication: 99999

Flow prefile sensitivity class: U10/D5

Environmental Class: Class B


Advantage:

1. High Precision Water Measurement. Account balance is 0, water supply will be stopped at once

2. Language and Currency unit can be customized.

3. Different alarm system according to market demand.

4. Data sheet can be sent to other management system.

5. A water meter can only input a user card, which ensures that even if the user reads the water meter incorrectly not cause losses.


FAQ

1. How to deal with the loss of user card

Please fill in the card in the software. After filling in the card, all the data of the user card in the system will be transferred to the new IC card.


2. Card Formatting

New cards opened in all systems need to be formatted before they can be used in the system.
