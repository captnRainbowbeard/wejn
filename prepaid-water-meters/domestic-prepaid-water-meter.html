Product Details

Domestic water meters are widely used in various houses, buildings and other places. As technology advances, domestic water meters continue to diversify. There are domestic mechanical water meters, domestic smart water meters, domestic remote water meters, etc. For more than 100 years, there has been no major technological innovation in the field of domestic water meter water supply. The debut of the home smart water meter can be said to be a major event that changed the history of water supply.


Parameters of domestic prepaid water meters:


Type:public , split type or integrated type


Size:15mm~25mm pipe fitting


Material:brass / plastic PPO


Power:electronic meter with mechanical register


Standard:ISO4064 class B


Application:purified water, fresh water

Although the prepaid water meter cannot have the function of transmitting data in real time like a remote water meter, it saves manpower costs to a certain extent. This feature is loved by most. If you want to know about the product, welcome to consult us.
