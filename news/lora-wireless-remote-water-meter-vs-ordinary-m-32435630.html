LORA Wireless Remote Water Meter VS Ordinary Mechanical Water Meter

Mar 19, 2020

Let's compare the current popular LORA wireless remote water meter with an ordinary mechanical water meter as an example.


The most prominent disadvantage of ordinary mechanical water meters is its meter reading method, and the impact of this disadvantage is increasingly prominent. In our inherent thinking, it is considered normal and normal for meter readers to go from house to house, but under the impact of technologies such as the Internet of Things, the efficiency of door-to-door meter reading methods has been questioned. The LORA wireless remote water meter developed using new technology is a meter reading instruction issued by the meter reader through the remote meter reading system. After the LORA module on the wireless remote water meter receives the meter reading instruction, it will upload the water meter. The data is uploaded to the server, and the meter reader can read the water meter data. This avoids the high cost, low efficiency of manual meter reading, and the problem of nuisance caused by household meter reading.


No matter what kind of water meter and what function it has, it is born to serve people's lives. Whoever can provide more convenience and efficiency will be put on the stage of history.
