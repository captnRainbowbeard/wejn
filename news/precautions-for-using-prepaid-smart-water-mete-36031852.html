Precautions For Using Prepaid Smart Water Meters

Jul 15, 2020

As a product of adapting to the development of the times, the prepaid smart water meter satisfies our development needs, brings earth-shaking changes to our life development, and creates more benefits. However, there are some matters that need to be paid attention to before the reasonable application and development of the product. Below we will introduce you in detail:


The purpose of installing a pre-paid water meter is to measure the amount of water, then when the pre-paid water meter does not produce water, it must be a headache. When this phenomenon occurs, there are three situations: 1. The balance in the IC card is exhausted, 2. The battery is dead, 3. The prepaid water meter is malfunctioning. Any problems found should be discovered and dealt with in a timely manner. Normally, you should pay attention to the "hygiene" of household prepaid water meters. Swiping prepaid water meters is good to say, especially card prepaid water meters. Once you do not pay attention to hygiene, some debris, dust or garbage fall into the card slot, causing the IC card to be inserted. Inefficiency affects our water use. When the pre-paid water meter is too low and the balance is too low, we should promptly pay to the management department. Don’t wait to close the valve. If we don’t have time to pay, it will delay normal water consumption. When winter comes, add a "coat" to the prepaid water meter to prevent frost cracking. Normally educate children not to intentionally destroy or play with prepaid water meters, but to raise children’s awareness of water conservation from an early age.


After the pre-paid water meter is installed, the pre-paid water meter can be used normally. In the process of using prepaid water meters, some problems will inevitably occur, so everyone should pay more attention in the normal use process. In order to prevent inconvenience to our household water.


Such reasonable maintenance helps the prepaid water meter bring us more convenience, make our life develop better and create more benefits.
