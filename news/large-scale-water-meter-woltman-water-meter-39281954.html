Large-scale Water Meter Woltman Water Meter

Oct 30, 2020

If you have a requirement for a large-scale water meter capable of measuring large flows, you need the LXLC/LXXG water meter. The LXLC/LXXG adopts a Woltman measuring style, designed to handle broad ranges of flow and is available in sizes 50-600mm.  

 

LXLC/LXXG have cold type and hot type. The cold type has a maximum temperature rating of 50°C, making it suitable for cold water applications.  The hot type has a maximum temperature rating of 90°C, making it suitable for cold water applications.  

 

High accuracy and performance are guaranteed with the woltman water meter, it is fitted in the horizontal position.  

 

It is worth noting that the woltman water meter is fitted with a PN16 flanged connection.  

 

The addition of Pulse output gives the meter remote data communication so readings can be picked up by a BMS or AMR system.
