Shang Hong's Travel Notes

Apr 28, 2021

Thanks for meeting in Shanghong, we carried out an outdoor outing group building activity together. Although the weather was bad before going out, there was still a drizzle. But we still go out as planned.

We went to eat hot pot chicken that everyone loves, and went to Longquan Lake Park to play together.In the park, everyone walked along the cherry blossom road together, watched the fish playing in the river together, sat on the lawn to chat, played games, and took pictures together. . . . . .


Working at Shanghong, everyone is very happy. We all work hard when we are at work, but there will also be relaxing moments when we are together. Looking forward to our next team building activity.
