Introduction And Advantages Of Prepaid Water Meters

Dec 16, 2020

SH-MECH IC card water meter supporting prepaid management system is composed of computer software, IC card, and water meter. The computer software system is easy to operate, friendly interface, safe and reliable, and has good confidentiality. Water supply companies can perform step-by-step billing according to different local conditions. Set up computer terminal management in residential quarters.

IC card water meter features:

1.Card issuance, user information management, water sales fee management, query, design.

2. Make reports, backup data, and print receipts.

3. Unique data encryption.

4. Hierarchical management of operators and classified management of users.

5. IP68 waterproof design, all electronic components are sealed with food-grade professional electronic potting glue, with high waterproof performance, safe and reliable.
