Differences Between Domestic Smart Water Meters And Domestic Mechanical Water Meters

Dec 25, 2019

There are thousands of varieties of domestic water meters, nothing more than smart water meters and mechanical water meters. Because the specifications we produce are different, we also have a wide range of domestic water meters, which makes people dazzled. Both are water meters, so what's the essential difference between them? Which water meter do users prefer?


After investigation, 80% of users prefer to use smart water meters, while 20% of users still prefer traditional mechanical water meters. The reason why 20% of users like traditional mechanical water meters is that mechanical water meters do not use batteries. Smart water meters require lithium batteries, and lithium batteries need to be disassembled to replace the water meters.


Let's talk about the difference between a home smart water meter and a refueling mechanical water meter. In fact, the most important point is that the smart water meter can more accurately measure the usage and form the water fee. In addition, the smart water meter also has a storage function, which can make the calculation of water charges to the greatest degree of accuracy. The smart water meter can directly exchange data with the business office, reduce the workload of the staff, and improve the quality of life. The user can Paying through mobile apps and other methods greatly reduces the added value and allows people more time to enjoy life. Advanced intelligent water meters are fully in line with the fast-paced life of modern people and can bring more convenience and speed to people's lives. The mechanical water meter requires the administrator to read the meter, which is very inconvenient to enter the household!
