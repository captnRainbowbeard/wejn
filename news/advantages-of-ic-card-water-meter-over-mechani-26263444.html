Advantages Of IC Card Water Meter Over Mechanical Water Meter

Aug 14, 2019

Mechanical water meter manages water resources by "first using water, then paying" mode. With this mode, many problems arise, such as difficulties in meter reading, arrears of water charges, etc. This will undoubtedly make it difficult for water supply departments to return funds to cages and work inefficiently. At the same time, it will form contradictions with users such as paying fees, checking tables and liquidating, resulting in supply and demand. Neither side is in a favorable situation. Comparatively speaking, IC card water meter manages water resources by pre-purchasing mode of "paying first, then using water", which not only does not require manual meter reading in the household, but also enables users to purchase water resources according to their own time schedule, which greatly improves the work efficiency of water supply units and provides convenience for users.


Above is the advantage of IC card water meter compared with mechanical water meter. It can be seen that IC card water meter is more conducive to water company management and service, and also brings convenience to users. Therefore, IC card water meter will replace the traditional mechanical water meter and become an indispensable necessities of life for people.
