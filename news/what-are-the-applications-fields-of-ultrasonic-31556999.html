What Are The Applications Fields Of Ultrasonic Water Meters?

Feb 14, 2020

The areas with relatively high demand for ultrasonic water meters include agricultural irrigation, township water supply, urban water supply, and smart city construction. The specific reasons are as follows:


1. Agricultural irrigation: no mechanical transmission parts, no flow-blocking components, ultrasonic water meter measurement is almost unaffected by water quality, can adapt to poor conditions of agricultural irrigation water quality, and is the most suitable product for agricultural irrigation water measurement;


2. Smart city: No pressure loss, reduce pump energy consumption, achieve energy saving goals; complete communication interface; convenient remote monitoring of the network, reflecting intelligence; battery-powered, no mechanical transmission parts, long service life;


3.Urban water supply: low starting flow rate of ultrasonic water meter, avoiding small flow and non-metering, increasing water fee income; high measurement accuracy, reliable guarantee of accurate measurement of water fee; wide range ratio, suitable for various users with different water consumption


4.Township water supply: adapt to various domestic water quality conditions; convenient communication networking, multiple output signals, convenient automatic settlement and computer management, to achieve centralized management of water resources, improve management efficiency, remote monitoring, and reduce management costs.
