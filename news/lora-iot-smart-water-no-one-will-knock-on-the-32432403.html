LoRa IoT Smart Water, No One Will Knock On The Door And Copy The Water Meter

Mar 19, 2020

In the past, when reading the meter, the user had to choose to read the meter during the weekend at home. When you go to read the meter, maybe they are still sleeping. He was awakened by a knock at the door, opened the door unkemptly, and looked at the meter reader in an awkward and vigilant manner. The disturbed user was completely sleepless ... Are you interested in learning about LoRa? What does the Internet of Things have to do with you?


In fact, the Internet of Things here can be understood as a network of LoRa base stations connected to your home's water meter, your neighbor's water meter, and many others' water meters. Of course, the Internet of Things has applications in many fields. LoRa is a network of water meters in the network. One line connected to the base station. In fact, the use of LoRa technology in the Internet of Things has been very mature, such as smart meter reading, smart parking, vehicle tracking, pet tracking, smart agriculture, smart industry, smart cities, smart communities, and other applications and fields.


The LoRa Internet of Things communication solution is an Internet of Things solution for smart meter reading, and this solution has changed the way of meter reading in the past when a door was opened. An LoRa base station can cover a few hundred thousand square meters In large-scale communities, even if the water meter is the farthest from the base station and the most severely blocked water meter, it can still realize the stable return of meter reading data and the stable distribution of control instructions.


It can be seen that technology can not only liberate the labor force, but also liberate your sleep. Improving the awareness of the Internet of Things can also feel more technologies that make life better. In addition to the construction of smart water affairs, the Internet of Things can have more application fields. Perhaps your next rediscovery of the Internet of Things will be another earth-shaking change in your life.
