Water Meter Installation Method

Nov 18, 2020

Water meter installation method:

1. When choosing a water meter, you need to choose a water meter with the same caliber according to the caliber of your water pipe.

2. The water meter is installed according to the horizontal direction, usually with the surface facing upwards, and the arrow in the water meter should be in the same direction as the faucet. And before installing the water meter, make sure that there are no other impurities in the water pipe to avoid shortening the service life of the water meter.

3. Valves should be installed at the upstream and downstream of the water pipe, and the valve should be opened when in use. If it is not used for a long time, the valve should be closed.

4. In the upstream and downstream of the water pipe, it is necessary to install a certain length of straight pipe section, and eddy currents will appear at some bends. At this time, a rectifier should be added in the front.

5. The water outlet downstream of the water meter must be 0.5m higher than the water meter, so as to ensure that the water meter counts more accurately.
