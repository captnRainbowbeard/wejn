How To Remove The Dirt Of IC Card Smart Water Meter?

Jun 07, 2019

Many friends who have used IC card smart water meter will pay attention to, generally after long-term use of the water meter, will produce some scales, and these scales formed in the IC card water meter can easily lead to water meter measurement errors, and even may lead to its transmission errors. Therefore, regular maintenance and regular cleaning are the necessary maintenance steps for the use of IC card water meter.


When IC card smart water meter is in use, attention should be paid to the cleanliness around the smart water meter, to avoid the corrosion of some acid and alkaline substances to the water meter, and to ensure the moisture-proof during the use, which will lead to the error caused by the damage of IC card water meter, and even the damage of IC card water meter.


If there is dirt in the water meter, it can only be said that it is a problem of water quality. Generally speaking, the method of turning on a large tap can be chosen to remove dirt. If the scale is blocked, the water meter can only be replaced. Never open the lead seal of the water meter privately, because only professional maintenance personnel can accurately judge the problem of the water meter. Private lead seals will only cause unnecessary losses to users.
