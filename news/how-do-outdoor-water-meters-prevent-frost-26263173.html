How Do Outdoor Water Meters Prevent Frost?

Jul 06, 2019

Cold Protection of Outdoor Water Meters

1. Making cold-proof mat for water meter, you can choose straw bag, snakeskin bag or homemade cotton mat. You can also buy glass wool mat to prevent cold and moisture.

2. Cleaning and repairing the inside of the water meter well so that there is no water accumulation or leakage in the water meter well.

3. Cover the outside of the upper cover of the water meter well and the surrounding of the water meter well with dry slag


Cold-proof of outdoor water pipes

1. Water tap antifreeze can be wrapped with cotton, linen or straw rope.

2. The bare water pipes at the entrance of the staircase of the unit are fastened with wooden boards or buckle boards, leaving a certain gap, and filled with wood chips, asphalt glass wool and other fillers.

3. Extremely cold weather can open the faucet in the house, so that the water flow of the water pipe can avoid the freezing of the water pipe.
