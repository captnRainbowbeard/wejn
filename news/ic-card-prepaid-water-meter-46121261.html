IC Card Prepaid Water Meter

May 27, 2021

IC card prepaid water meter, one water meter and one IC card, once the water meter is not charged, the valve is automatically closed. After recharging, swipe the card in the card reading area to complete the recharging, and then restore the water supply.


We provide a complete set of systems, water meters, IC cards, card readers and software. The software does not depend on the network, it can be used even without a local area network, and the operation is convenient. 


If you have difficulty charging in your area, please contact us, and we will provide you with a suitable plan based on your situation.
