NB IOT Smart Water Solution

Jan 19, 2021

The water consumption data, electricity quantity, signal and abnormal conditions of intelligent water meter can be connected to NB IOT network through NB IOT communication module built in water meter, transmitted to IOT connection management platform, and then uploaded to the background collection and business system platform; the background system platform will analyze the data packet, and the resolved user gas data will be settled in the user account, which will be sent through the customer service system Users can get their water bills in real time and recharge their accounts remotely

NB IOT intelligent water solution function


It can solve the problems of meter reading difficulty for water companies and recharge difficulty for users

It can help water companies actively respond to the government's remote water price adjustment and system ladder water price setting

The background business system can remotely monitor the fault and alarm of the intelligent water meter and feed back to the user

By analyzing the water quantity data of each node, we can further investigate the pipeline running, emitting, dripping, leaking, stealing and other problems, and realize the partition measurement, so as to reduce the supply and marketing difference of the water company and improve the efficiency of the water company

Through the analysis of users' water consumption data, users' water consumption habits can be known, so that other value-added services and business models can be mined
