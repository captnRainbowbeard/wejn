Wireless Remote Water Meter--LORA

May 25, 2020

The wireless remote transmission water meter adopts advanced wireless transmission technology, and the microelectronic control circuit converts the measurement information of the traditional mechanical water meter into an electrical signal and stores it. The measurement data can be automatically copied through the wireless remote network, and the data can also be copied to the back-end system through the wireless meter reading machine.

 

Technical characteristics

• Protection grade: IP68;

• Low power consumption design;

• No wiring, simple construction and convenient maintenance;

• Flexible upload frequency setting: data upload cycle can be set;

• Large-capacity storage: can freeze up to 24 months of monthly data and 31 days of frozen data;

• The system uses B / S architecture: just log in with a browser;

• Mobile WeChat applet: login, usage query, online payment.
