Detailed Problems That Tend To Occur When Installing A Water Meter

Nov 18, 2020

Detailed problems that tend to occur when installing a water meter:

1. If it is a newly laid pipeline, before installing the water meter, you need to clean up the debris in the water pipe, so as not to cause the water meter to move slowly or even stop moving when it is used in the future.

2. Before installing the water meter, it is necessary to understand the direction of water flow in the pipeline, and then ensure that the arrow on the water meter is consistent with the direction of water flow during installation. This is more convenient for the later use of the water meter and the data will be more accurate

3. There will be a dial on the water meter. When we install it, we should keep it level and install the dial upwards. If the installation is uneven, it will increase the frictional resistance between the water meter wing wheel shafts, which will reduce the sensitivity of the water meter, and eventually lead to inaccurate measurement data. The more inclined the water meter is installed, the slower the water meter will go.

4. If the water meter is installed outdoors, it is necessary to install a protective box for the water meter, so that the water meter can be protected from the sun and rain, the service life will be longer, and the measured data will be more accurate.
