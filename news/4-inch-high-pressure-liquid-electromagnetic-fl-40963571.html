Knowledge of ultrasonic heat meter

Dec 21, 2020

In office buildings, commercial buildings, and shopping malls, the measurement of heat and cold is essential, especially for countries in tropical regions. Here SHMETERS meter is willing to share some knowledge of our ultrasonic heat meter with you. Hope to help you find a solution.

structure

Ultrasonic heat meter, including ultrasonic transducer assembly and measuring tube.

A box seat is set on the base of the meter box, the box seat cover is matched with the box seat cover, and the heat meter probe is set in the box seat cover; M-bus collector and M-bus collector are set on the integrator, control circuit board, and control circuit board Bus communication interface.

Features

1. Can be installed horizontally and vertically.


2. High temperature measurement accuracy and long service life.


3. It can measure heat, cold quantity, and cold heat at the same time.


4. The flow meter sends out a pulse signal proportional to the flow rate.


5. A pair of temperature sensors give out analog signals that indicate temperature.


6. Calculate the amount of heat obtained by the heat exchange system using the cumulative formula.


advantage



（1) Low cost of comprehensive use: no mechanical impeller rotation, no mechanical wear, low cost of later use and maintenance, and a long service life;


（2) Good measurement reliability: The small impurities passing through the front filter of the heat meter will not affect the accurate measurement of the ultrasonic heat meter;


（3) Less metering disputes: When the ultrasonic heat meter is used, it is not blocked, not worn, and the metering is accurate, which is beneficial to the smooth progress of the heat metering work;


（4) Convenient maintenance: Ultrasonic heat meters are basically maintenance-free products.
