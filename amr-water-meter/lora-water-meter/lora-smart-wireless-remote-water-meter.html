Product Details

The wireless water meter LoRa water meter adopts advanced wireless communication technology and code verification technology, which has the characteristics of high sensitivity, strong anti-interference ability and long transmission distance.


Features:


1.Remote control valve: water meter can realize remote control valve function through the system.


2.Prepayment function: the water meter can realize the prepayment function through the post-order system and software platform.


3.Low battery alarm: when the battery in the water meter is too low, the system can be used to alert.


4.Each table has an address mark to ensure the accuracy of the data.


5.The data adopts advanced data encoding and verification technology, and the communication reliability is high.


6.Flexible configuration, the wireless water meter can be networked without setting after leaving the factory.
