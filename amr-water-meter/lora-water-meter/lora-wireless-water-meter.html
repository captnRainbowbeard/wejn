LoRa Wireless Water Meter

The wireless water meter has the characteristics of high sensitivity, strong anti-interference ability and long transmission distance.

Send Inquiry
Product Details

The wireless LoRa water meter is an important device that constitutes a wireless meter reading system. It has an address mark to ensure the accuracy of data transmission. Using advanced wireless communication technology and code verification technology, it has the characteristics of high sensitivity, strong anti-interference ability and long transmission distance.


Features:


1. Micro power consumption design, built-in lithium battery, service life is more than 6 years.


2. Each table has an address mark to ensure the accuracy of the data.


3. The data adopts advanced data encoding and verification technology, and the communication reliability is high.


4. Flexible configuration, the wireless water meter can be networked without setting after leaving the factory.


5. Communicate in the international public frequency band, with strong penetration.


6. Adopting narrow-band communication technology, high sensitivity and strong anti-interference ability


7. This product has valve control function (optional).
