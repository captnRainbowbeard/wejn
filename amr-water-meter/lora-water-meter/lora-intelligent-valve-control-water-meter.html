LoRa Intelligent Valve Control Water Meter

Product Details

The intelligent LoRa water meter uses a smart valve-controlled water meter as the base meter, and embeds optoelectronic devices, single-chip microcomputers, communication modules, and control circuits in the water meter counter box.



Product Features


1. Water meter data is reported periodically


The water meter can regularly report data to the server. The reported data includes accumulated water consumption, battery voltage value, valve status, meter status, and sampling parameters. The periodic reporting period can be specified by the user.


2. Quantitative reporting of water meter data


Whenever the consumption reaches a multiple of the specified value, the water meter can automatically upload data to the server. The reported data includes accumulated water consumption, battery voltage value, valve status, meter status, and sampling parameters. The quantitative reported value can be specified to meet the collection requirements of the water supply company for special users.


3. All wireless data communication:


LoRa wireless water meter, GPRS concentrator, meter reading and charging platform, and all devices transmit data wirelessly. The construction is simple, there is no wiring difficulty, the installation is quick and simple, and it is suitable for most environments.



4. Each LoRa water meter has an ID number. In addition to using a concentrator for wireless communication, it can also be equipped with a handheld device for identification and control collection.



The remote valve-controlled water meter series developed based on LoRa wireless communication technology.LoRa is based on linear frequency modulation spread spectrum modulation, which can greatly increase the communication distance. It also has the characteristics of fast networking speed, strong environmental adaptability, simple structure, ultra-low power consumption, and strong real-time performance.
