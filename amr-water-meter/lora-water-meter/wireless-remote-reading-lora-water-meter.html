Wireless Remote Reading LoRa Water Meter

The water meter can monitor the operation and usage of the meter at any time, and is suitable for remote meter reading and control of civil water meters in various building structures such as multi-storey and high-rise slabs, towers, villas, and apartments.

Send Inquiry
Product Details

Features:

1. Point-to-point communication distance can reach 2KM.

2. Spread-spectrum modulation is adopted, which has strong anti-interference ability to ensure effective and stable data transmission;

3. To achieve frequency division multiplexing, it is possible to copy several networks of the entire area with one frequency;

4. Adopt non-magnetic transmission design to avoid magnetic adsorption and prolong the service life of mechanical parts;

5. The system is based on LoRa communication technology, adopting a simple star-shaped network structure, the communication delay is small, the transmission distance is long and reliable;

6. Synchronous communication time unit; FM technology avoids co-frequency interference to improve transmission reliability, adaptive transmission rate and transmission distance algorithms, and effectively increase system capacity

7. No complicated construction wiring is required, the amount of work is small, the concentrator and the water meter form a star-shaped network, and the concentrator forms a network with GRPS / 4G and the back-end server. The network structure is stable and reliable.
