Product Details

Smart LoRa water meter DN50mm


This water meter, 15mm or 20mm in diameter, is available for cold water or hot water. It allows the measurement of water consumption at regular intervals, and the transmission of data via the LORAWAN data network (public or private). It is supplied with a digital display, and offers an electronic protection against electromagnetic fraud.
The water meter allows reading and sending data at different frequencies. It is possible to store locally up to 200 measure of consumption.


Use case of the LORAWAN water meter

  • Measurement of water consumption in buildings or apartment

  • Industrial water consumption measurement

  • Measurement of water consumption in smart cities

LORAWAN Water Meter Technical Specifications

  • Reading optoport

  • Data collection every 1, 6, 12, 24 hours

  • Data transmission every 1, 6, 12, 24 hours

  • Measurement of the ambient temperature

  • Battery powered (up to 10 years)
