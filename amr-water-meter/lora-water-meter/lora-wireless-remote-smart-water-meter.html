Product Details

The wireless remote water meter LoRa is a smart water meter that has appeared in recent years. The characteristic of the water meter is that it can realize remote meter reading without wiring. At present, the technology of LoRa wireless remote meter is relatively mature.



Performance characteristics of wireless remote water meter LoRa:


1. Low cost: use free SIM wireless measurement frequency band.


2. Reliability of data link: The coding adopts highly efficient cyclic interleaving error detection coding.


3. Low power consumption design: passive transceiver, high-capacity lithium-ion battery power supply, battery life of more than 6 years.


4. Diversity of meter reading modes: You can use the network meter reading method of the concentrator network, or you can use the wireless hand meter reading machine to read the meter while walking.


5. Long-distance transmission: In a complex urban environment, it also has a coverage of 1.2km. For long-distance nodes, it is connected by a multi-layer routing network.


6. Strong anti-interference: Adopting the unique spectrum widening processing technology of LoRa, the terminals of different spreading sequences will not interfere with each other even if the same frequency is sent at the same time.
