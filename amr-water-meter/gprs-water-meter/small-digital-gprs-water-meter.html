Small Digital Gprs Water Meter

The GPRS water meter is equipped with a GPRS module, which can integrate the usage data and wireless communication of the water meter into a water meter.

Send Inquiry
Product Details

GPRS water meter works by integrating two modules of data collection and wireless communication into one water meter. GPRS water meter adopts GSM / GPRS technology, which reduces the cost and risk, and improves the stability and reliability of water pen work. Not only that, it can also communicate with a smartphone to set water meter parameters and check all usage data.

Product Features:

1. Ladder water price

The water meter can achieve a stepped water price, that is, the water fee is different at each stage, which can effectively prevent water waste.

2. GPRS / GSM communication

GPRS / GSM is a relatively stable communication technology. Water meters can be installed anywhere, and the communication cost is low.

3. Automatically upload data

Water meter data can be automatically uploaded and sent to the system until the data is successfully uploaded.

4. Voltage monitoring

The battery voltage will be monitored in real time, and the high and low voltages will remind the user.
