Home Commercial Residential GPRS Water Flow Meter

The Home commercial residential GPRS water flow meter developed by sh-meters , the smart water meter system consists of a GPRS water meter, a SIM card and an operation management platform (web version).

Product Details

GPRS smart water meter is a product developed by shmeters specifically for the service of apartment buildings, suitable for decentralized apartments and buildings. It has a valve control function to regularly clean dirt and not easily block the pipeline. 


Advantage:

1. The installation is simple, and the mechanical water meter is installed in the same way, without wiring;

2. It is not affected by the length of communication distance, and the signal transmission is stable;

3. In the normal time, all sleep state, only to reach the online time will be online 30S, to ensure battery life.
