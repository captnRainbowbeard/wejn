Residential Intelligent AMR GPRS Water Meter

The residential intelligent AMR GPRS water meter is totally R&D by sh-meters , the smart water meter system consists of a GPRS water meter, a SIM card and an operation management platform (web version).

Send Inquiry
Product Details


The Residential Intelligent GPRS water meter uses AMR technology to realize remote  transmission of data. On the one hand, the GPRS wireless remote water meter can achieve accurate measurement of the water meter, which can measure the speed of water flow. On the other hand, it can achieve "indoor meter reading", and the manager can copy the water meter data at the operation console.
