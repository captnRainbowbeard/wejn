Product Details

GPRS water meter uses 2g communication and can be used normally in most countries.our  GPRS  water meter have widely used in many countries,let me make a brief introduce of our product.

1. The GPRS water meter system sends and receives signals from the water meter through 2g signals. The SIM card module is built in the water meter. You can read and control the water supply through the computer.


2. The water meter is easy to install and no wiring is required. The installation method is the same as that of a mechanical water meter.


3. The transmitted signal is not affected by the communication distance, and the signal transmission is relatively stable.


4. The water meter usually sleeps. Only after reaching the online time, it will continue to be online for 30S to ensure the battery life.
