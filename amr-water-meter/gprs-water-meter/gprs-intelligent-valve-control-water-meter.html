GPRS Intelligent Valve Control Water Meter

GPRS water meter adopts advanced micro-power wireless technology. The wiring is simple and the meter reading distance is long.

Send Inquiry
Product Details

GPRS water meters do not require on-site debugging when installing equipment. This function greatly improves the quality of the project, reduces the cost of wiring construction sites, and can also avoid disputes. Users can take meter readings remotely through repeaters and concentrators, and can also read meter readings through mobile phones and handhelds.


GPRS product technology:


1. The GPRS wireless remote transmission valve control function can facilitate the management department to realize pre-charging, or remote control, remote recharge and other functions.


2. It can realize the tiered water price, which is convenient for the management department to charge according to the tiered water price.


3. In order to ensure the service life and accurate reading of the sensor, the wireless remote water meter adopts the Hall effect sensor. In the circuit design, it can not only judge the forward and reverse of the water meter, but also monitor the cumulative amount of the water meter reverse, and ensure the mechanical degree of the water meter. Always consistent with electronic readings.


After the water meter is packed, it should be transported by vehicles without strong vibration. It will not be directly affected by rain, frost, fog, etc. during transportation.
