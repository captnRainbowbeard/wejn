Product Details

The intelligent GPRS automatic cold water meter belong to high-tech products in the smart water meter, using of advanced wireless transmission technology, it transforms metered information of conventional mechanical water meter into electrical signal have it stored by micro-electronics control circuit. Intelligent GPRS automatic cold water meter is able to automatically read the metering data via wireless remote network and is also able to transfer the data to the back-office system (data center) after reading the meters.


Power supply: 2.1V-3.6V

Accuracy Class: Class B

Normal Diameter: DN15-DN600

Maximum indication: 999999.9

Flow prefile sensitivity class: U10/D5

Environmental Class: Class B


FAQ

1. What is the battery life?

Regular use of ER26500 battery, 8500mAh, the frequency of meter reading once a day, battery life can be up to 4 years.


2. What is the transmission distance?

In the case of ensuring the GPRS signal strength, the GPRS water meter upload data is not affected by the distance;


3. Protection level?

At present, the GPRS wireless water meter adopts the full potting and sealing technology, which uses the silicone gel package circuit board part, and the battery plugging port is also sealed to achieve the IP68 protection level.


4. Can the product soak in water?

This product can soak water, but the water will affect the stability of signal transmission, because the signal will be greatly affected in the water transmission.
