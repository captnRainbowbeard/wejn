Installation Requirements and Maintenance of Water Meter

Aug 14, 2019

1. Before the water meter is installed, the sediment in the pipeline should be emptied to prevent the water meter from being blocked.

2. See clearly the flow direction of the water meter to prevent reverse loading.

3. During installation, the length of the straight pipe at the intake end of the water meter shall not be less than 10 times the length of the caliber of the water meter, and the length of the straight pipe at the outlet end shall not be less than 5 times the length of the caliber of the water meter.

4. Installation location should be considered to avoid corrosive areas and unobstructed drainage areas.

5. A water stop valve should be added behind the installed water meter to prevent the return of water in the pipeline behind the water meter from causing damage to the water meter or polluting the water supply network.
