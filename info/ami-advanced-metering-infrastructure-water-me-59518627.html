AMI（Advanced Metering Infrastructure) water meter

Jul 22, 2021

AMI is a two-way communication based on the Internet of Things. Providing continuously available two-way communication between the network and the metering equipment, supporting functions such as precise measurement, collection of billing information, demand feedback, remote service connection and disconnection. 


Unlike AMR, AMI does not require utility personnel to collect data, and the system will automatically transmit data directly to the utility at predetermined intervals.


AMI can be seamlessly integrated with large-scale advanced leak detection and pipeline assessment platforms to create a truly smart city. To put it simply, AMI can develop with the upgrade of a certain system.
