Technical Parameters and Performance Characteristics of Rotor Single-stream Dry Water Meter

Jul 18, 2019

Type: Pure water meter; Accuracy grade: B level; Minimum reading: 30; Nominal pressure: 1.6 (MPa); Nominal diameter: 15 (mm); Structural form: Dry water meter.


Characteristics of Rotor Single-stream Dry Water Meter:

Rotor single-flow dry-type water meter adopts magnetic drive, vacuum seal, anti-condensation atomization never pollution, long-term maintenance dial shows clear value, counter has the function of arbitrary rotation, which brings convenience to reading water meter, wide measuring range, small starting flow, reliable structure, durable, compact size and so on.
