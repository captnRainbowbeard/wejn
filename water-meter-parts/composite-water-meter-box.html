Composite Water Meter Box

Water meter box is a box used to protect the water meter, which can appropriately extend the life of the water meter

Send Inquiry
Product Details

Water meter tanks are commonly used civil infrastructure equipment and are widely used in civil infrastructure construction in towns, large, medium and small cities. The structure of the water meter box is as follows: the back of the box is an open structure; the back of the side wall of the box is provided with a water inlet pipe gap through which the water inlet pipe passes; A small box door and a large box door, and observation windows and billing holes can be configured according to actual needs.


Features:


1. The color of the water meter box is mostly gray or blue, and the appearance is simple;


2. The cabinet has good corrosion resistance, acid and alkali resistance, and impact resistance;


3. Long service life, indoor service life can reach 30 ~ 60 years, outdoor use can reach 15-30 years;


4. The box material has low recyclable value, which effectively prevents theft;


5. The box is not cracked and deformed, easy to use and simple to maintain;


6, the water meter box needs to be equipped with multiple water control valves.
