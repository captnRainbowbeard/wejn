Product Details

The leakage rate is always an important index that affects the economic benefit of water supply enterprises. The average city in the world has a leakage rate of 30% and up to 40%. The main reason for the high leakage rate is the high initial flow rate of the mechanical rotary water meter (hereinafter referred to as the mechanical water meter). In the process of actual water use, users often make the mechanical water meter work under the critical or lower starting flow state, resulting in the loss of water consumption and the increase of leakage rate.
