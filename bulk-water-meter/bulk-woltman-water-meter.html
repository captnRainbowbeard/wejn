Bulk Woltman Water Meter

Reducing diameter can effectively rectify and achieve high precision measurement.

Send Inquiry
Product Details

Advantage

1. Reducing diameter can effectively rectify and achieve high precision measurement.

2. Reducing diameter can effectively improve the measurement of small flow rate of ultrasonic water meter and improve the measured range of water meter.

3, reducing diameter can really realize the ultrasonic water meter without front and rear straight pipe section installation requirements.

4. Reducing the diameter can effectively improve the stability of the flow field, and make great achievements in the long-term reliable measurement of the ultrasonic water meter

5. Reducing diameter can reduce the sound path of ultrasonic beam, reduce power consumption and improve the service life of ultrasonic water meter.


Installing:

1. To determine the diameter of the water meter, should first understand the size of the flow in the pipe, to the often used flow close to the commonly used flow is appropriate, can not be simply determined by the diameter of the water meter;

2. The installation should avoid the location with strong mechanical vibration influence and avoid being close to the noise source;

3. The installation site shall avoid exposure to the sun, freezing, pollution (such as strong acid, strong alkali, mineral oil and other chemical corrosive substances) and long-term flooding, so as not to affect the disassembly and meter reading;

4. The water meter and upstream and downstream straight pipe section to coaxial installation, should ensure that the water meter measurement pipe axis and pipe axis direction, the measurement pipe axis and horizontal line Angle is not more than 3°, sealing gasket shall not burst into the pipe, so as to avoid inaccurate measurement;

5. Ultrasonic water meter can be installed horizontally and vertically, when the vertical installation requires water flow from the bottom up.
